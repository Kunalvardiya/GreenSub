About the Project

Rethinking Waste with AI

GreenSub helps users correctly sort waste using AI-based image recognition. When a user uploads an image, our model assesses whether the item can be reused or resold — or should be sent for proper recycling.

If the item has market value, it's listed on our green marketplace for nearby buyers. If not, we connect it with local trash collectors and recycling companies like Gravita India.

Making a Difference

Our Environmental Impact

Every year, millions of tonnes of recyclable material end up in landfills simply because people don't know how to sort their waste correctly.

GreenSub bridges this gap by using AI to classify waste in real-time, route reusable items to buyers on our marketplace, and connect recyclables with certified recycling partners.

By keeping items in circulation longer and ensuring proper recycling, GreenSub reduces landfill load, lowers carbon emissions, and promotes a circular economy.
